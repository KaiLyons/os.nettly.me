Nettlos_

Nettlos_ is a bunch of operating systems based on Ubuntu (Some in the future might be Arch based) and all made by a stupid teenage developer by the name of Nettly_.
The goal of Nettlos_ is to simply keep Nettly_ busy when she has nothing to do.
Nettlos_ is made with Ubuntu 19.04 and basically removes the original GUI and adds new ones. The first ever launch was a beta version on September 18th, 2019 with LXQt as the first ever DE to come with Nettlos_... what an honor I guess.
This project is not really made to share just being shared so Nettly_ has something public.
